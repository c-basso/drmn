## Full Description

Sound Machine Deep Sleep DRMN is an iOS sleep audio app for adults who want calming sounds for sleep, relaxing sleep music, and deep rest support on iPhone and iPad. Use this app to block disruptive noise, unwind at bedtime, and wake up more rested.

**Who it is for**
- Adults who search for sounds to sleep, sleep music, or deep sleep audio to fall asleep faster
- Parents who need baby sleep sounds and gentle white noise for infants and toddlers
- Light sleepers who want steady background audio through the night
- Anyone replacing a bedside sound machine with a portable iOS option

**What the app does**
Play continuous white noise, brown noise, pink noise, rain sleep sounds, ocean waves, thunderstorms, and nature ambience. Layer multiple tracks in the sound mixer to build a personal soundscape, then let the sleep timer fade audio out automatically.

**Sounds for sleep and deep rest**
- Listen to sounds to sleep to, fall asleep sounds, and soothing audio designed for bedtime
- Use free white noise for sleep and deep sleep sounds to reach deeper rest
- Run nature sounds for deep sleep and fan noise overnight with battery-friendly playback
- Mask street noise, snoring, and sudden household sounds so you stay asleep longer

**Sleep music and relaxation**
- Calm instrumental-style sleep music and peaceful bedtime audio in the same library
- Switch from deep work or study mode to wind-down listening without changing apps
- Meditation sounds and ambient layers for stress relief before bed

**Baby and family sleep**
- Soothe infants with familiar rhythmic audio such as hair dryer, vacuum cleaner, and gentle static-style sounds
- Create a calming nursery soundscape without buying a separate baby sound machine
- Help toddlers settle with steady background audio parents control from one app

**Powerful features**
- Mix multiple sounds at once to create your ideal soundscape
- Set a sleep timer with gradual fade-out so playback stops on its own
- Download sounds for offline listening during travel or poor connectivity
- Control volume and layers with a clean interface built for bedtime use
- High-quality recordings designed for sleep, deep sleep, and relaxation

**How sounds for sleep help**
A steady audio backdrop spreads sound across frequencies so sudden noises are less likely to wake you. Consistent sleep sounds help your brain unwind, shorten time to fall asleep, and support longer, more restorative deep sleep.
