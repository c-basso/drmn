About DRMN

DRMN is a freemium iOS app that helps you fall asleep faster and stay focused with white noise, brown noise, rain, fan sounds, and other ambient audio. It is designed for iPhone (iOS 17.1+) and available free on the App Store.

Developer

DRMN is built by Vladimir Ivakhnenko, an independent iOS developer. You can view his other apps on the Apple App Store developer page.

What makes DRMN different

  • Generous free tier: one-hour sleep timer, offline playback, and sound mixing
  • 100+ hours of professionally recorded ambient and nature sounds
  • Baby sleep sounds (hair dryer, vacuum, keyboard-style tracks)
  • Battery-optimized for all-night use without draining your iPhone
  • No subscription required to try core features
