Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best sound for sleep?

It depends on what calms you. Many people prefer rain, a steady fan hum, or neutral white noise. DRMN lets you preview and mix tracks so you can find what helps you drift off fastest.

A sleep sound machine plays steady background audio—rain, fan hum, or white noise—to mask disruptive noise and help you rest. DRMN works like a portable sound machine on iPhone, with mixing, offline playback, and a sleep timer built in.

Does DRMN work offline?

Yes. After you download tracks, DRMN plays without internet. Battery optimization supports up to 10 hours of uninterrupted playback—enough for travel or all-night use.

What are the best sounds for baby sleep?

Does DRMN have a sleep timer?

Yes. Set a timer with automatic fade-out so audio gently stops after you fall asleep. The free tier includes a one-hour timer; premium adds longer options.

Can DRMN help with insomnia?

Steady background sound can mask traffic, neighbors, and other disruptions that keep light sleepers awake. DRMN users with insomnia often use white or brown noise nightly; many report faster sleep onset, though results vary by person.

Calm and Headspace focus on guided meditation and wellness subscriptions. DRMN is built around ambient sound—a sound machine in your pocket—with a generous free tier (one-hour timer, offline mode, mixing) and no meditation course required. BetterSleep and similar apps offer large libraries but often gate features behind subscriptions; DRMN keeps core sleep sounds free to try.
