BetterSleep Alternative for iPhone (2026)

BetterSleep (formerly Relax Melodies) is one of the most downloaded sleep sound apps on iPhone, known for a large library of mixes, meditations, and bedtime content. DRMN is a leaner sleep sound machine app focused on masking noise, offline playback, and a free tier that includes mixing and a sleep timer.

If you searched for a BetterSleep alternative, you probably want either lower cost, fewer paywalls, or a simpler sound-machine experience. This guide compares both apps factually for sleep-only use cases.

Why people look for BetterSleep alternatives

  • Subscription fatigue: BetterSleep premium is typically ~$59.99/year (U.S., 2026) for full library access.
  • Feature gating: Free users often hit limits on sounds, mixes, or session length.
  • Complexity: Large catalogs and upsells can feel heavy if you only need steady rain or fan noise.
  • Battery use: All-night playback with many layers can drain battery on older iPhones.

When DRMN is the better BetterSleep alternative

DRMN fits if you want a straightforward sound machine without navigating a large premium catalog:

  • You mainly need white, pink, or brown noise plus rain, ocean, or fan sounds.
  • You want to mix two or three layers without paying monthly.
  • You travel often and need reliable offline playback.
  • You use a sleep timer with fade-out and do not want audio running all night.
  • You prefer a smaller app footprint and battery-friendly overnight use.

When BetterSleep still wins

BetterSleep remains strong if you value:

  • Hundreds of pre-built mixes and frequent new content drops.
  • Integrated bedtime meditations and breathing programs.
  • A mature ecosystem you already paid for and customized over years.

Migration tips: switching from BetterSleep to DRMN

  1. Identify your top two sounds in BetterSleep—e.g., heavy rain + distant thunder.
  2. Recreate the mix in DRMN with rain + brown noise at low volume.
  3. Download tracks for offline use before your first night.
  4. Set a 45–60 minute timer with fade if you only need help falling asleep.
  5. Keep volume moderate (40–50 dB)—louder is not more effective.

Key takeaway

BetterSleep is a content platform; DRMN is a tool-first sound machine. For sleep masking on iPhone with a usable free tier, DRMN is a practical BetterSleep alternative. If you rely on guided sleep programs and a massive preset library, staying with BetterSleep may still make sense.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to BetterSleep on iPhone?

DRMN offers a free tier with a one-hour sleep timer, offline playback, sound mixing, and core white noise and nature sounds without requiring a subscription to try basic sleep features. BetterSleep gates much of its library and mixing behind premium plans.

Is BetterSleep the same as Relax Melodies?

Yes. BetterSleep is the rebranded name for Relax Melodies, a long-running sleep sounds app with a large catalog and subscription model.

Does DRMN have as many sounds as BetterSleep?

BetterSleep advertises a larger total catalog. DRMN focuses on professionally recorded ambient, nature, and noise-color tracks with emphasis on mixing quality and offline battery-optimized playback rather than catalog size alone.

Can I mix sounds in DRMN for free?

Yes. Sound mixing is included in DRMN's free tier. BetterSleep historically limits advanced mixing and full library access without premium.

Which app is better for travel and offline sleep?

DRMN is optimized for downloaded tracks and long offline sessions on a single charge. BetterSleep supports offline downloads for subscribers, but the experience is built around unlocking premium content.
